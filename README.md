# 🪐 Planetrix - Explore the Cosmos

Welcome to **Planetrix**, an immersive, interactive journey through our solar system. Designed with love for space enthusiasts and explorers alike, Planetrix brings the wonders of the universe right to your screen with a premium, celestial aesthetic and fluid animations.

## 🚀 The Experience

Planetrix isn't just a website; it's a cosmic experience. We've built an interface that feels alive—from the subtle rotation of orbital rings to the gentle floating of distant moons.

- **Interactive Navigation:** Seamlessly glide between planets with smooth, physics-based transitions.
- **Dynamic Data:** Each celestial body comes with its own unique stats, themes, and companion elements.
- **Atmospheric Design:** A dark-mode first interface with glassmorphism, vibrant glows, and blurred space vistas.
- **Skeleton Loading:** We hate abrupt jumps. Experience a smooth skeleton transition as the data orbits into view.
- **Mobile First:** Whether you're on a desktop at NASA or on a phone in your backyard, the cosmos fits in your pocket.

## 🛠️ Built With

We used a modern tech stack to ensure the stars align perfectly:

- **[React](https://reactjs.org/)** - For the robust UI foundation.
- **[Framer Motion](https://www.framer.com/motion/)** - The engine behind our "out of this world" animations.
- **[Tailwind CSS](https://tailwindcss.com/)** - For that sleek, utility-first styling.
- **[React Router](https://reactrouter.com/)** - Navigating through space at the speed of light.
